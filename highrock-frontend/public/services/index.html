Our Services

High Rock has two distinct divisions

High Rock started as an Institutional Asset Management firm managing funds for Scotiabank but then added on our Private Client Division. Although we no longer manage money for Scotiabank, the construct exists and it provides synergies for us to manage money for certain special mandates, some for our Private Clients while others are for Endowments, Family Trusts and Corporations.

Private Client Wealth Management
At High Rock's Private Client division, we manage money for individuals, families, professional and private corporations on a Separately Managed Account (SMA) basis.
High Yield and Special Mandates
We manage several high yield bond mandates for corporations, family trusts, endowments and some high net worth individuals
